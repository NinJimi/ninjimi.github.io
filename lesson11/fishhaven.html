Killer Ducks Roaming Fish Haven

by Viktor Koshnov

Officials are warning the people not to go fishing this season as a flock of killer ducks have migrated north this summer. The ducks have been seen with webbed feet as sharp as knives and teeth as jagged as that one road we all hate traveling down. If you find yourself confronting one of these beasts in the wild it is recommended to throw a loaf of bread at it. It will distract the duck long enough for you and your family to run away. Since this is the best stragety known so far in dealing with the ducks, officials have been sent out to distribute one loaf of bread per family as protection. Be aware that these ducks are very dangerous. There have been 30 injuries and 12 deaths recorded so far as of this article being written. Take precaution when going out to the lake this season. If you see any duck approaching in a threatening manner please save yourselves and run as fast as you can.
