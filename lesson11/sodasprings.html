Camp Connor Closed

by Martha Stewart

Camp Connor has been closed off to the public this summer due to an unforseen invasion of aliens. The aliens showed up on March 17th, 2021 sporting green hats and shirts with four leaf clovers. They have apparently come to celebrate St. Patty's day with us. Unfortuneatly the government will not allow us to party hard with the aliens. Since we have not met them in this holiday for partying, they have taken great offense and have since set up encampents all over the area. We won't know how long they stay but we have been advised not to go near them, as we do not know what kind of diseases they may be carrying. If you still want to go camping this summer we would recommend going anywhere else these aliens have not marked as their territory. They have shown no violence towards anyone who gets near, but the government has put up limits around the encampent.

In other news, buffalo have started walking on their hind legs and we have no clue why. Scientist are disturbed at this most recent phenomenon and are trying to find out if it is in relation to the aliens arriving on earth. They say it started happening only shortly before the aliens have arrived. This new habit the buffalo have assumed is making videos of their acts go viral. One man recorded two buffalo doing the cha cha dance just a couple miles outside of the closed off alien camp. Some strange things are going on here. Hopefully things will be going back to normal soon so that we can all enjoy our summer vacations.
